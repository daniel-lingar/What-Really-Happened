Core Deployment Rule

A signed contract does not authorize deployment. Readiness does.

WRH does not run at a site unless the site can identify support leads, backup pathways, escalation procedures, documentation chains, and follow-up responsibilities before the pilot begins.

Site Readiness Gate

The readiness gate determines whether a site is Approved, Approved with Conditions, Deferred, or Not Approved for current deployment. Sites without a support pathway are deferred before activation.

Closed-Loop Support Bridge

A referral is not a connection. WRH tracks whether a participant is connected, stabilized, transferred to an agency pathway, or documented and escalated as unresolved.

Pilot Operating & Evaluation Package

The pilot package is the evidence layer. It contains the pre-registered pilot framework, field forms, activation logs, support bridge logs, site performance scoring, final pilot report, and revision decision memo.

It answers the next reviewer question: Can WRH be run safely once, measured honestly, and improved before it scales?

Claims Boundary

Risk governance and pilot evaluation support operational claims only. They do not support claims that WRH treats PTSD, prevents suicide, treats addiction, reduces clinical symptoms, or replaces licensed care.

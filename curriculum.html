What Lives Here

The source repo defines WRH as a 75-session master curriculum organized into Part I, Part II, Part III, and Part IV. This live portal turns that raw curriculum structure into a public navigation layer for buyers, reviewers, and implementation partners.

WRH remains: not therapy, no disclosure, non-clinical education.

Deployment Control

Curriculum architecture alone does not authorize deployment. WRH deployment is controlled by risk governance, site readiness, support pathways, and pilot evaluation.

What Really Happened™ (WRH)

Capability Statement

Non-clinical trauma education, curriculum architecture, implementation support, risk governance, and pilot evaluation infrastructure for courts, agencies, veteran-serving programs, and community partners.

Capability Curriculum Operations Risk Governance Source Map

Operational Boundary

WRH is a structured, plain-language, non-clinical trauma education system. It does not provide therapy, diagnosis, clinical treatment, medical advice, legal advice, or crisis response. WRH does not require, request, or encourage participants to disclose personal trauma history.
